FAQ

What is Dach.dev?

Dach.dev is a digital platform committed to making the profound teachings of Chabad Chassidus accessible to all. Offering a user-friendly learning experience, with a focus on simplicity, our interface allows seamless study of Chassidus directly from your phone or computer.

Can I contribute to Dach.dev, and if so, how?

Absolutely! We are a community built platform. Whether you're a software developer, designer, or are able to help with digitizing/adding Seforim to our library, your contributions are vital. Engage with us, share your thoughts, and be a part of the Dach.dev community. Contact us at info@dach.dev to get involved!

How does Dach.dev ensure the accuracy of Chassidus translations?

At Dach.dev, we utilize a custom AI model to translate Chassidus content for our users. While we strive for accuracy, it's important to note that these translations are not perfect. Despite our best efforts, errors may still occur. We encourage our community to help us identify and correct any inaccuracies they encounter. Reporting an error is easy – simply right-click on a paragraph or long-press on a mobile device. Your contributions play a vital role in improving the quality of our translations over time.

Is offline access available?

Although Dach.dev is primarily an online platform, we have recently introduced a printing option, which allows users to print a full text in an easy-to-read layout. Additionally, we're exploring other methods, such as eBooks, text downloads and an offline website.

How often is new content added?

The frequency of new content depends entirely on the efforts of volunteers and contributors. Your invaluable contributions help us steadily expand the collection, and reach our goal of bringing every piece of Chassidic literature to the world. stay tuned for regular updates on our growing repository of Chassidic teachings.
